The MELHS Theorem

What is the MELHS Theorem?

The MELHS Theorem states that the MELHS Roof Functionality on a scale of 1-10 multiplied by the MELHS Internet Speed in a value of 1-10 will always equal the MELHS Constant

How We Calculate It.

We first find the MELHS internet speed (S). We then convert it to a corresponding value from 1-10. By dividing the MELHS Constant (C) by our converted Internet Speed, we receive our Roof Functionality (F) output on a scale of 1-10.
